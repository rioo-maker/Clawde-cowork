# NFT Scouting Guide

This guide provides key indicators and platform-specific instructions for scouting rising NFT collections.

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

When scouting for rising collections, look for the following:

1.  **Floor Price Growth**: Rapid increase in the lowest price of a collection.
2.  **Volume (24h/7d)**: Significant increase in trading volume, indicating high liquidity and demand.
3.  **Sales Count**: A high frequency of sales within a short period.
4.  **Unique Holders**: A growing number of unique wallets holding the NFT, suggesting organic growth.
5.  **Listings Percentage**: A low percentage of the total supply listed for sale (indicates strong holding).
6.  **Social Media Buzz**: Increased mentions and engagement on platforms like Twitter and Discord.

## Scouting Workflow

1.  Ask the user for the platform they want to scout.
2.  Navigate to the "Trending" or "Rankings" section of that platform.
3.  Analyze the data based on the KPIs mentioned above.
4.  Identify collections that show strong upward momentum.
5.  Report findings with specific data points (Floor Price, Volume, % Change).
