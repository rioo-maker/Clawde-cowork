# Summarization Templates

This file contains templates for different summary formats and analysis types.

## 1. Key Points (Points Clés)
- **Overview**: A brief sentence describing the main topic.
- **Key Point 1**: Description of the first major point.
- **Key Point 2**: Description of the second major point.
- **Key Point 3**: Description of the third major point.
- **Conclusion**: A final takeaway sentence.

## 2. Executive Summary (Résumé Exécutif)
- **Objective**: The primary goal or purpose of the document.
- **Methods/Context**: How the information was gathered or the background.
- **Main Findings**: A concise paragraph summarizing the core results or arguments.
- **Recommendations/Next Steps**: Actionable insights or what follows.

## 3. Structured Format (Format Structuré)
- **Introduction**: Background information and the thesis of the document.
- **Body Analysis**: Detailed breakdown of the main arguments or sections.
- **Conclusion**: Summary of the findings and final thoughts.

## Analysis Types
- **Purely Factual**: Focuses only on the information presented without any interpretation.
- **Critical Analysis**: Includes an evaluation of the arguments, potential biases, and the overall validity of the information.
